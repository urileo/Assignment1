Project Ideal: 3D Virtual Assistant

Overview

I want to present to you guys my brand new, highly advanced technology that I’m sure it would become a true thing in the future: Io - The 3D Virtual Assistant. In the future, there’d be a lot of new inventions that do not exist at present such as: automated flying transportation, human cloning (might be?),... and many more. Therefore, this project will be an integral part of our life, our future so I hope this “product” would spread all over the world in order to get widely used by everyone.


Motivation:This project will definitely be well-known once its first establishment. I can tell you why it would so interesting. This 3D VA (or Io) is like a far beyond advanced version of other VAs nowadays: Google Assistant, Siri, Alexa, and so on. Why would I say it like a “far beyond advanced version”. Well, it’s because Io will do almost everything for you from basic needs to fundamental demands. For example, when you get home tired, you don’t want to do anything but having a nap, you just need some commands to tell Io do that for you, ready the bath, prepare the cooking materials, light on/off, that is some basic things Io can do.

Description
First of all, we should wonder what is Virtual Assistant? If we don’t know what it is then how can we get interested in it, right? A Virtual Assistant (VA) is an application that can understand voice commands and complete tasks from a user. Moreover, it can also do everything from answer questions, tell jokes, play music and control your household items. Not only that, the VA can send text messages, make phone calls, or set up reminders itself for you whenever you ask.
Next, we will find out more about this product – Io virtual assistant. With the combination of using Artificial Intelligence (AI), Io can understand natural language, even a joke you tell to Io, she will respond back as naturally as human do. That is the most sophisticated part which is what we expect Io to answer.
Of course, not only comprehending but Io can also recognize images or faces. You don’t want to have a VA with an advanced AI recognize a thief breaking into your house as it’s owner and cannot tell the difference of voices, right?
In contract, what you expect from Io is talking to you about whom she saw and what they did. Then what about your daily routine, your work schedule? Instead of spending time on planning those, you have Io do it for you with just a few talks. Why do I use the word “talks”, not commands? It’s because just like I said, Io is a far beyond advanced version of other VAs, so using “commands” makes user feel a bit unnatural and forceful, right? The evolution of AI inside Io makes her blend in human communication, so “talks” seems closer.
For example, you are about to have an appointment tomorrow, instead of saying: “Hey Io, set up an appointment at 4.30pm tomorrow”, you can say: “Hey Io, could you set up for me an appointment at 4.30pm tomorrow to discuss on....?”. Although both of it will have the same output, the first one just respond: “Oke, I just made it for you” then the sencond respond will surprise you more than that: “Oke, what else do you want me to do?, Do I need to....” or it could suggest some ideas for you to show how flexible Io is. Another one, whenever you feel sad or discourage, Io will automtedly cheer you up by some of her jokes, maybe?.
That’s what I call communication, not just commanding and responding only, or not just a mimic human behavior anymore. Io has knowledge itself to learn how to have a conversation exactly what humans do. That’s the core, what about the “vessel”? Because Io is a 3D VA, her appearance must depend on ProVision 3D media, which is known as 3D-hologramme to display interactive object but without the 3D glasses. Here, the object or the model we have is like a human to get friendliness from the user. Every movement will just like a human. However its flexibility shows that Io can appear anytime, anywhere you need; when you got home, Io shows up; when you are out, Io will accompany with you in mobile phone or any electronic devices that Io can access. Convenience, right? You won’t get lost if Io constanly remind you where you are or calling an ambulance when you suddenly get sick. Io is no longer a normal AI which just encode commands and execute it anymore because by putting her into real-life situation and see the reaction, she is already unique.

With the hardware, it had the system integration (SI) to deal with a great deal of data and many other types of equipments.

For the software, she needs 2 programming languages: Python and HTML, python is used for the core, the other is used to develop the chatbox.
Cloud API storage memories and data. Moreover, voice, images recognition are required, which are programmed to interact with user frequently.

Skills Required
- An expert Python developer would more successfully to develop AI Virtual Assistant. The reason is Python is used as a base for the most renowned AI-based software because of its flexibility, simplicity and longstanding reputation. Moreover, depending on open source is a good, fast and conveninent way to program such libraries and tools as: NumPy, Matplotlib, Pandas, Scikit, Theano, AIMA, pyDatalog, SimpleAI, EasyAi, PyBrain, MDP, scikit, PyML and others.
- Analising statistics and data is also important. It helps the VA predict or contemplate environment attributes and react( respond back) the situation.
- Voice, images recognition, objects detection and weather prediction programs are used to give feedbacks for owner whenever they need. If this happenned, daily life would be very convenient. You can imagine that when the weather is about to be rainy, snowy, or windy then VA will show you the weather condition and suggest bringging raincoat, thick clothes or umbrella and so on.
